Biography

Yuan Zhang is a Ph.D. candidate at CFCS, Peking University, advised by Prof. Shanghang Zhang and Prof. Kuan Cheng. Previously, he received his M.Eng. degree from Peking University and B.Eng. degree from Hohai University. He has interned at SenseTime Research, Alibaba DAMO Academy, ByteDance Seed, and UC Berkeley BAIR.

His research focuses on multimodal large language models, with an emphasis on interpretability and efficient inference, as well as efficient AI techniques, including knowledge distillation, pruning, and data selection. He has published at top-tier venues including NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, and CVPR.
